Our Professional Services

From refreshing your home's exterior to complete interior renovations, we deliver quality craftsmanship on every project.

Professional Painting & Exterior Care

Interior & Exterior Painting

Transform any space with our professional painting services. We use premium paints and meticulous preparation techniques to deliver flawless, long-lasting finishes for walls, ceilings, trim, and exteriors. Our color consultation service helps you choose the perfect palette for your home.

Decks

Create the perfect outdoor living space with a custom-built deck. Whether you're looking for a cozy retreat or an expansive entertainment area, we design and construct durable, beautiful decks using quality materials that withstand the elements year after year.

Fences & Pergolas

Define your property with style and security. Our fence installations provide privacy, safety, and curb appeal, while our custom pergolas create elegant outdoor structures perfect for shade, entertaining, or simply enjoying your backyard in comfort.

Power Washing

Restore your home's exterior surfaces to their original beauty. Our professional power washing removes years of dirt, grime, mold, and mildew from siding, driveways, patios, decks, and walkways, instantly improving your home's appearance and protecting surfaces from damage.

Staining & Wood Restoration

Bring weathered wood back to life and protect it for years to come. Our staining and restoration services enhance the natural beauty of decks, fences, siding, and outdoor furniture while providing essential protection against moisture, UV damage, and wear.

Concrete

From patios and walkways to driveways and foundations, our concrete services deliver durable, attractive results. We handle new installations, repairs, and decorative finishes including stamped and stained concrete to enhance your property's functionality and appeal.

Pavers & Deck Sealing

Protect your investment with professional sealing services. We apply high-quality sealants to pavers, decks, and outdoor surfaces that guard against staining, fading, and weather damage while enhancing colors and extending the life of your hardscaping and wood structures.

Interior Remodeling

Kitchen Renovations

Create the kitchen of your dreams with our comprehensive renovation services. From modern cabinet installations and elegant backsplashes to new flooring and countertops, we transform outdated kitchens into functional, beautiful spaces where your family will love to gather.

Bathroom Remodeling

Transform your bathroom into a personal retreat. Our full-service bathroom renovations include tile work, vanity installation, shower and tub upgrades, and complete layout redesigns. We handle everything from minor updates to complete gut renovations with attention to every detail.

Basement Finishing

Unlock the hidden potential of your basement. We specialize in basement finishing, waterproofing, and creative layout conversions that transform unused space into comfortable living areas, home offices, entertainment rooms, or whatever your family needs most.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services and process.

How do I get started with a project?

Getting started is easy! Simply call us at 716-545-4454 or fill out our contact form to schedule your free on-site estimate. We'll visit your home, discuss your vision, assess the scope of work, and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote. There's never any pressure—we want you to feel confident in your decision.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. BEE Pro is fully licensed and carries comprehensive liability insurance to protect you, your home, and our team. We're also EPA certified for lead-safe work practices and proud to be BBB accredited. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.

How long will my project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical interior paint job might take 2-5 days, while a full kitchen renovation could take several weeks. During your estimate, we'll provide a realistic timeline and keep you informed throughout the process. We pride ourselves on completing projects on schedule without sacrificing quality.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve homeowners throughout the greater Buffalo area and surrounding communities. If you're unsure whether you're in our service area, give us a call—we're always happy to discuss your project.

Do you offer financing options?

We understand that home improvements are a significant investment. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your budget. We're committed to making quality home improvement accessible to all homeowners.

What makes BEE Pro different from other contractors?

We combine professional expertise with genuine care for our customers. Our team consists of skilled craftsmen who treat every home as if it were their own. We communicate clearly, show up on time, maintain clean work areas, and stand behind our work. Our EPA certification and BBB accreditation reflect our commitment to the highest standards in the industry.

Can you help me choose colors and materials?

Yes! We offer complimentary color consultations as part of our service. Our experienced team can help you select paint colors, stain finishes, materials, and design elements that complement your home's architecture and your personal style. We'll bring samples and provide expert guidance to help you make confident choices.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We maintain open communication throughout every project and conduct a final walkthrough with you to ensure everything meets your expectations. If any issues arise, we address them promptly and professionally. Our reputation is built on happy customers, and we're committed to earning your trust and referrals.
